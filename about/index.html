ABOUT FORSEN

Who is Forsen? Hans Eli Sebastian "Forsen" Fors, is a Swedish roleplayer, livestreamer, and Stockholm-based hobo extraordinaire. This multitalented gaming guru first found fame in the digital realm with the release of Starcraft 2, where he swiftly conquered the competition as a formidable Zerg player. With the strategic finesse of a Starcraft 2 veteran, Forsen navigated his way into the Hearthstone scene, stealthily climbing the ranks to become the top Rogue player across multiple regions. His Hearthstone success was just the beginning, as his Twitch stream quickly gained traction and attracted a loyal following.

After bidding farewell to Hearthstone, Forsen ventured into a variety of livestreamed gameplay, showcasing his diverse skill set in games like PUBG, Atlas, and Minecraft Speedrunning. Known for his uncanny ability to turn any game into a laugh riot, Forsen's humorous commentary [citation needed] is as legendary as his gaming prowess.

Forsen currently resides in a Stockholm insane asylum, where he enjoys the company of his pet rat, Peppah, and his imaginary girlfriend, Nina. When he's not breaking records or entertaining his fans, Forsen can be found pondering life's greatest mysteries, such as how to speedrun his way out of the asylum.
